The Anti-Trash Manifesto

Modern formats like .jpg and .mp4 are fundamentally broken. They rely on rigid pixel grids that blur when zoomed and waste massive amounts of data. VectorVision introduces a coordinate-based paradigm, delivering infinite resolution and 85% data savings.

.svgc

Replacement for: JPG, PNG, BMP

The Scalable Vector Geometric Camera protocol. Captures reality as mathematical paths. Perfect for high-fidelity images that never blur.

.svgv

Replacement for: MP4, MKV, AVI

The Scalable Vector Global Video standard. A stream of geometric data that allows for crystal-clear playback on the slowest networks.

Core Technology

  • Anti-Trash Fidelity Lock: Real-time edge preservation for high-interest zones like facial features.
  • Parallel Scanline Vectorization: C++/NDK engine optimized for budget mobile hardware.
  • Coordinate-Based Storage: Files that are inherently searchable and infinitely scalable.
